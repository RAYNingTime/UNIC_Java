1. Which of the following method calls are legal?
Not legal are: 
    d - Because we are trying to call a function with an int with double variable
    e - Because we are trying to call a function with an int with double digit
    g - Because we are trying to call a function with variable that is not created
    i - Because we are trying to call a function with a single argument, while second doesn't have any default data

Legal are: a,b,c,f,h,j
